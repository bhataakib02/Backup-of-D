About CineStats

CineStats is an educational project inspired by Box Office Mojo, focused on the All-Time Box Office section. It is built with Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and vanilla HTML/CSS/JS.

Data is demo/sample for non-commercial use. You can seed the database with included sample JSON. Credits to public sources and open-source community.
